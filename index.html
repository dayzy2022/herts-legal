Services

Criminal Defence

We are here to help you if you are suspected or accused of a crime. When the police allege that you have committed a criminal offence, we can represent you to make sure your legal rights are protected and to present your case in court to make sure you get a fair hearing. We will use knowledge and experience to ensure the best outcome for you and your case.

Property

We are here to handle all the legal aspects of buying or selling a property. We will keep you updated regularly, and can support you by answering questions about the process of buying or selling, handle contracts, give you legal advice, carry out local council searches, deal with the Land Registry, and transfer the money to pay for your property.

Wills and Probate

For many of us, drafting a will is hardly at the top of our to-do list. But the fact remains that the process of drafting your will, gives you the clarity and reassurance that your estate is going to be appropriately and fairly dealt with when you are no longer here, putting you and your loved ones at ease. Our experienced team go above and beyond to make the process as stress-free and easy as possible.

Family

The family is, for most people, the centre of their lives. So, when conflicts arise, or things change, it is natural for a great deal of emotion to arise. Our family law solicitors can assist with pre-nuptial agreements, divorce settlements, cohabitation agreements and childcare proceedings. We are here to represent you in any formal legal proceedings, including family court, and negotiate the best outcome for you and your family.
